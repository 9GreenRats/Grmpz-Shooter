The best creations are born out of vibes, or so they say.

Grmpz (Grumpiez) are experimental, grumpy, nearly almost hooman characters by me on a random day.

Initially, they were created to be a part of my Project Alfies' Alfieverse, these characters had the potential to transcend their original purpose.

Their grumpiness was not just a personality trait but a core part of their identity. Who are they? This is a journey to finding out!
